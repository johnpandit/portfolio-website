No Regrets

Hypothesis

Connect the tattoo community, both enthusiasts and artists in one place in order to display the bests tattoos in the world.

SWOT and Competitive Analysis

Tattoodo was used as a compeitior. They had strengths such as connection to artists and inspirational options. Overall the weaknesses such as no location data inputed, overload of information which outweighed the strengths. The project was going to move forward to create a simple tattoo community app.

LoFi Design

Create lo fidelity designs of all of the main fuctions of the app. Lowering the cost of design changes and provide a better plan moving forward.

A/B Testing

Once a final design was made, further refinement was done using a simple preferance survey.

Polished Design

Final polished design of the Jump page was created compling all of the research and data. This page will be seen after going through the insight tutorial.
